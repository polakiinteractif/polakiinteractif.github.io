We do not collect, store, or process any personal data or usage data.

The App may access certain device features (e.g., controllers, headset tracking, VR environment rendering) solely to provide core functionality. No data from these interactions is collected or transmitted.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, tracking tools, ads, or external SDKs that collect user data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Since no data is collected, the App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your region).
